  • 2Open the GL Mapping screen for the applicable property.
  • 3Review each GL code against the client's chart of accounts.

📍 How to Find Occupancy Data in RealPage

Follow these steps to locate your budgeted occupancy percentages in RealPage:

  1. Log into RealPage and navigate to the Budget module.
  2. Select the applicable property from the property list.
  3. Open the Budget Summary or Occupancy tab.
  4. Locate the row labeled Budgeted Occupancy or Economic Occupancy.
  5. You will see monthly percentages listed January through December — enter those values here.
  6. Enter as a whole number percentage (e.g. enter 92 for 92%).
If you cannot locate this data, contact your Regional Manager or Budget Support team.
